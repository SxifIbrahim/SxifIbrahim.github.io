Terraria Update Timeline

Terraria, a sandbox adventure game developed by Re-Logic, has seen numerous updates since its release in 2011. The game's timeline is marked by significant content expansions and quality-of-life improvements.

  1. 1.0 1.0 was the release version of the game.
  2. 1.1 The 1.1 patch was the largest update to date in terms of new content before the 1.2 update. It introduced Hardmode, the Wall of Flesh, and the mechanical bosses.
  3. 1.2 The 1.2 update introduced a massive amount of new features, as well as balances to Hardmode gameplay.
  4. 1.2.1 The 1.2.1 patch, also known as the Halloween Update, added several new features, fixed various bugs, and made various game changes. It made Halloween Terraria's second seasonal event (after Christmas) and introduced the Pumpkin Moon.
  5. 1.2.4 The 1.2.4 patch introduced fishing, including Fishing Poles, bait, fish, various crates, tools, weapons, accessories, and junk that can be "caught", the Angler NPC, various fishing gear, fishing quests, and fishing quest rewards. Also, a new boss Duke Fishron and a Pixel Piracy crossover.
  6. 1.3 The 1.3.0.1 update, commonly referred to as 1.3, adds a large quantity of new items and characters, major new bosses and events, the brand new Expert world mode, and many significant changes to the game's mechanics and interface. A large number of the game's existing elements have also been adjusted for balance and gameflow.
  7. 1.3.1 The 1.3.1 release, also known as the Wiring & Mechanics Update, focused largely on wiring mechanics and controller support.
  8. 1.3.3 1.3.3 focuses on updates for the Desert and Underground Desert biomes.
  9. 1.3.5 The 1.3.5 update focuses on language translations, 4K support, Zoom and UI scale, and new furniture.
  10. 1.4 The 1.4.0.1 update, named Journey's End, is a major update that was released on May 16, 2020. The release occurred on Terraria's 9th anniversary, with May 16, 2011 being the original release date. It has been declared by Re-Logic staff as the final major feature update to Terraria.
  11. 1.4.3 1.4.3 focuses on a content crossover between Terraria and Klei Entertainment's Don't Starve Together.
  12. 1.4.4 1.4.4 is an update that focuses on balance and quality-of-life changes, but also includes a significant amount of new content. It was released on September 28, 2022.
